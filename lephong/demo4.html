OUR SERVICES

CUOR provides advanced computational fluid dynamics (CFD) solutions tailored to diverse architectural and engineering challenges, leveraging cutting-edge numerical methods to optimize airflow and enhance the built environment. We offer a comprehensive suite of services, encompassing simulation, consultation, and specialized testing, designed to create safer, healthier, and more sustainable spaces

CFD Simulation & Analysis

Our expertise extends to the detailed modeling and simulation of airflow within various contexts:

  • Green Building Design: We provide comprehensive CFD analysis of natural and mechanical ventilation systems in green buildings. This includes evaluating design effectiveness, optimizing energy efficiency, and ensuring occupant comfort and improved indoor air quality. Our simulations encompass a variety of building typologies and climate zones.
  • Industrial Workplace Safety: We analyze and optimize mechanical ventilation systems in industrial facilities, specifically focusing on creating a safe and healthy working environment for employees. Our simulations assess airflow patterns, contaminant dispersal, and thermal comfort to mitigate occupational health hazards.
  • Fire Safety Engineering: We employ CFD modeling to design and test fire protection systems across different building types and scales. Our simulations provide critical insights into smoke propagation, heat transfer, and evacuation strategies, ensuring maximized safety in case of fire emergencies.

Consulting & Training

We offer expert consultation and tailored training programs to support the broader adoption and effective application of CFD technologies. Our services include:

  • Architectural Design Guidance: Our team provides expert consultation on optimizing ventilation within architectural designs, offering insightful recommendations to improve indoor environmental quality.
  • Collaborative Project Support: We partner with architectural firms, educational institutions, and businesses to integrate CFD modeling seamlessly into their workflow, fostering collaboration and driving innovation.
  • Research & Publication Support: Our commitment to advancing CFD methodologies extends to the publication of original research articles in leading peer-reviewed journals. This endeavor expands the knowledge base and encourages industry best practices

Ventilation Quality Assessment

Our advanced simulation capabilities facilitate precise quantification and analysis of indoor environmental quality:

  • Large-Scale Environment Assessment: We utilize CFD to model and simulate air quality within large-scale environments, such as commercial areas, historical buildings, and spacious rooms. This involves meticulous quantification of parameters like wind velocity, temperature, and humidity profiles, allowing for identification of potential design flaws or areas needing optimization.
  • Personalized Comfort Analysis: For residential settings, we assess air quality within bedrooms and identify optimal air conditioning placement customized for the individual, mitigating risks associated with respiratory or musculoskeletal diseases. Our approach addresses the personalized thermal and airflow needs for healthy environments

Real Estate Marketing & Development

Our proficiency extends beyond design analysis into practical applications within the real estate sector. We provide tailored marketing and support website services:

  • Climate-Specific Ventilation Designs: Our CFD expertise aids the development of ventilation systems optimized for each specific geographic area and climate, resulting in better living spaces aligned with environmental considerations
  • CFD-Based Marketing Strategy Website: We generate sophisticated CFD visualizations and performance data to build websites that effectively showcase superior ventilation and indoor air quality, boosting the marketability of real estate projects emphasizing these features. This includes creating compelling visual representations to demonstrate a commitment to well-being, energy efficiency, and sustainability in each building design
