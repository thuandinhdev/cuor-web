Navigating the Future: Autonomous Systems for A&D

The Emerald 68 building has been remodeled for investigating its natural ventilation in relation to local wind directions. The evaluation methodology was based on the Building and Construction Authority's (BCA) Green Mark (GM) Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) analysis from 2021. This paper aims to provide project investors with essential insights for evaluating and enhancing natural ventilation quality in buildings. However, it's important to note that this analysis was based on a reconstructed building model derived from estimations available on the website XXX. Therefore, the results only represent a partial assessment of the building's natural ventilation capabilities.
